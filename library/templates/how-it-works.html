How It Works

Tivan is a free-to-use web application that allows you to catalogue the videogames you own and even explore the collections of your gaming friends.

Have you ever been desperate to play a particular game but simply can't afford it? Maybe you've found that you & your friends have been laying your hard earned on the same games? We might be able to help you out....

Remember, the good ol' days when you & your friends used to loan & share your games with one another? Well, those days don't heave to be gone and Tivan is here to help you!

Using the application simple & intuitive interface you can catalogue your games and connect with other Tivan users you may know.

Sign Up

Signing up to use Tivan is quick, easy, and completely FREE!

It's a 3 step process starting with asking you for some standard login details. We will then ask you what game you're currently playing and if you want to connect with another Tivan user right off the bat.

Connect with Gamers

The application allows you to form a connection with another user you may know (some may call it 'friending....'.

By linking up with one another you can pool your game collections. Maybe your mate Pete has a game you've been dieing to play and you never knew. But Tivan knows. Maybe give Pete a holla and see if he'll let you borrow it....

The Library

Here's where a lot of the applications magic happens. Your library is made up of the game collections of all the users to whom you're connected all pooled together. Think of it like a 'virtual' Netflix of games!

The game and the platform your friend owns a copy for are clearly displayed. Just clicked on a title of you choosing, see who owns it and give them a nudge. They may be able to hook you up.

{% include 'svg/break_fw.svg' %}

Your Collection

Sometimes it's handy just to peruse your own collection. Well, Tivan lets you do that too.

Adding a title is simple. Just fill out the games title, select it's platform, tick or untick it's availability to other users, hit the button and BOOM it's there.
