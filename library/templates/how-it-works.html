How It Works

Tivan is a free-to-use web application that allows you to catalogue the videogames you own and even explore the collections of your gaming friends.

Have you ever been desperate to play a particular game but simply can't afford it? Maybe you've found that you & your friends have been laying your hard earned on the same games? We might be able to help you out....

Remember, the good ol' days when you & your friends used to loan & share your games with one another? Well, those days don't heave to be gone and Tivan is here to help you!

Using the application simple & intuitive interface you can catalogue your games and connect with other Tivan users you may know.
